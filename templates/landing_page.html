How it works

Up and running in minutes

No installation, no setup headaches. Create an account, add your products, and start selling.

1
Create your account
Register as an owner. Verify your email via OTP and your account is ready. Invite staff members by sharing your username with them.
2
Set up your catalog
Add your products, categories, and suppliers. Set prices, cost, and link materials to your inventory for real-time stock tracking.
3
Run your operations
Start a sale session, log expenses, record purchases, track waste, and review your daily profit summary — all in one place.
Features

Everything you need to operate

From ringing up a sale to reviewing end-of-day profits — Swift ERP covers the full business cycle.

Point of Sale
Create sessions, add products, apply discounts, and close with a full revenue summary including employee shift cost.
Inventory Tracking
Monitor raw material stock levels in real time. Know what's running low before it impacts your operations.
Purchase Management
Record supplier purchases with a cart-style flow. Track line items, quantities, and your year-to-date spending.
Expense Tracking
Log operating expenses from reusable templates. Filter by category and period to see exactly where money is going.
Waste & Loss
Record spoiled or wasted materials and products. Waste cost is factored directly into your daily profit summary.
Employee & Payroll
Add employees with daily rates, assign shifts to sessions, and auto-estimate your monthly payroll obligations.
Daily Summary
A full end-of-day breakdown: sales, purchases, waste, salary, expenses, and net profit — all in one view.
Supplier Catalog
Organize your raw materials by supplier with saved presets to speed up future purchase entries.
Dashboard Analytics
See revenue, expenses, and net profit at a glance. Quick access to all modules from one central hub.
Access control

The right access for every person

Role-based permissions ensure each team member can only see and do what they're supposed to.

Staff
Frontline team members who handle day-to-day sales and product tasks.
  • Create & manage sales
  • Browse product catalog
  • View inventory stock
  • Cannot access financials
  • Cannot delete records
Owner
Full control over every module, every record, and every team member.
  • Full access to all modules
  • View financial summaries
  • Manage employees & payroll
  • Create, edit & delete anything
  • Manage staff accounts
Developer
Read-only access for auditing the system or building integrations.
  • Read all records & data
  • View every module
  • Cannot create records
  • Cannot edit or delete
  • No write access
