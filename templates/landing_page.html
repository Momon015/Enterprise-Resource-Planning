How it works

Up and running in minutes

No installation, no setup headaches. Create an account, add your products, and start selling.

1
Create your account
Register as an owner. Verify your email via OTP and your account is ready. Invite staff members by sharing your username with them.
2
Set up your catalog
Add your products, categories, and suppliers. Set prices, cost, and link materials to your inventory for real-time stock tracking.
3
Run your operations
Start a sale session, log expenses, record purchases, track waste, and review your daily profit summary — all in one place.
Features

Everything you need to operate

From ringing up a sale to reviewing end-of-day profits — Swift ERP covers the full business cycle.

Sales
Ring up transactions in a clean session-based point-of-sale flow. Override unit prices on the fly when costs spike — apply a temporary markup without touching your catalog, then revert to default the moment costs settle.
Inventory
Stock updates itself. Materials from supplier purchases flow straight into inventory, with per-unit cost calculated automatically from pack size — no manual entries.
Purchases
Record supplier orders through a cart-style session. Apply discounts, adjust unit costs when prices rise, and let every purchase update your inventory and material cost in one step.
Expenses
Log operating expenses — utilities, rent, payroll, and more — from reusable templates. Filter by category and period to see exactly where money is going.
Waste & Loss
Log defective, expired, or self-consumed stock the moment it happens. Waste cost is factored straight into your daily profit so your numbers always reflect reality.
Employees & Payroll
Add employees, set daily rates, and log shifts — including backdated entries when you forget. Payroll flows into your expenses alongside utilities, rent, and other operating costs.
Daily Summary
A complete end-of-day breakdown — sales, purchases, waste, payroll, and expenses rolled into one net-profit view you can review in seconds.
Supplier
Organize raw materials by supplier and save recurring orders as presets — weekly and monthly restocks become a one-tap action instead of a manual entry.
Dashboard
Revenue trend, cost breakdown, and side-by-side period comparisons at a glance. Spot what's working — and what's draining your margins — without digging through reports.
Access control

The right access for every person

Role-based permissions ensure each team member can only see and do what they're supposed to.

Staff
Frontline team members who handle day-to-day sales and product tasks.
  • Create & manage sales
  • Browse product catalog
  • View inventory stock
  • Cannot access financials
  • Cannot delete records
Owner
Full control over every module, every record, and every team member.
  • Full access to all modules
  • View financial summaries
  • Manage employees & payroll
  • Create, edit & delete anything
  • Manage staff accounts
Developer
Read-only access for auditing the system or building integrations.
  • Read all records & data
  • View every module
  • Cannot create records
  • Cannot edit or delete
  • No write access
